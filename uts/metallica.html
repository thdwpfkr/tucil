metallica shirt

This piece is a tribute to Metallica, combining the band's legacy with my signature sigilism aesthetic. The back features medieval-inspired sigil ornaments, side portraits of the four band members, and references to iconic albums such as Ride the Lightning, ...And Justice for All, Reload, Death Magnetic, and Kill 'Em All. A coiling snake ties the composition together, adding a sense of power and movement. On the front, I reworked the Metallica logo with sigil-inspired elements, combining it with a coffin, lightning imagery, and descending typography. Through this design, I wanted to merge the raw energy of Metallica with the dark, mystical atmosphere of sigilism and medieval symbolism.
