reworked polo

I got this polo from someone's, and honestly, it was the first polo shirt Ive ever owned. I never really liked wearing polos before, but after trying this one on, I realized it actually looked good on me, and I ended up liking it. After that, I never wore it again until I started this brand. I decided to rework it by cutting off the collar because it wasnt symmetrical. Then I drew a sigilism artwork on it with romantic elements, which can be seen through the love symbolism in the design. Theres also a personal letter on the back dedicated to the person who gave me this, and to the person who helped me pour these feelings into the artwork. Thank uuu for giving me this shirt, I was able to rework it, and it turned out really cool, shout out to ruzi for making it happen.
