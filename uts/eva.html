boxy work jacket

Inspired by Evangelion, this piece explores themes of seraphim, divinity, and the ethereal through layered wings and symbolic eyes referencing SEELE, the unseen force behind the story's events. The phrase "The End of Evangelion. Finale: Take Care of Yourself" is included as a personal tribute to someone who once gifted me this jacket. Though the garment is gone, the memory remains preserved through the artwork.
